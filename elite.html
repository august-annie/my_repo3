ELITE PROPERTY LAW

This case study highlights Elite Property Law's first website design project, which aimed to establish a robust online presence in Brisbane's competitive property consulting market. Focused on user experience and transparency, the website was carefully developed to meet the needs of key user personas and offer valuable insights into local market trends and services.

Through easy-to-use navigation, clear communication, and thoughtful design strategies, Elite Property Law effectively positioned itself as a trusted authority, resulting in more website visitors, fewer people leaving the site quickly, and higher rates of turning visitors into clients.

Challenges

Elite Property Law encountered a significant challenge in establishing its presence in Brisbane's competitive property consulting market, worsened by limited financial resources. With numerous established firms competing for clients' attention, attracting potential customers proved daunting. Furthermore, the absence of an online presence hindered their ability to effectively reach and engage with their target audience. Additionally, as most clients rely on platforms like Google to find property consulting services, the lack of visibility on such platforms exacerbated the challenge of acquiring new customers and expanding their client base.

Solution

To address the challenges posed by limited financial resources and the need to improve online visibility and attract new customers, Elite Property Law implemented a comprehensive solution centred around the development of their first website. Drawing on their understanding of user needs, they created a user-friendly website that focused on transparency and accessibility. They ensured that the website provided detailed information on local market trends and services, including transparent pricing details, to build trust and credibility with potential clients. Moreover, Elite Property Law optimised the website for search engines like Google, boosting its visibility and increasing the chances of attracting organic traffic. Through these strategic initiatives, Elite Property Law successfully addressed the challenges posed by the competitive market and limited budget, establishing themselves as a trusted authority in property consulting and laying the foundation for future growth and success.

Approach

Elite Property Law embarked on creating their first website, emphasising user experience and transparency despite their limited budget. They carefully designed the website to resonate with their target audience, focusing on key user segments. By including details about local market trends, transparent pricing, and easy navigation, Elite Property Law aimed to build trust and credibility with potential clients from the outset. Additionally, the website served as a platform to showcase their expertise and set themselves apart from competitors, giving them a competitive edge in the Brisbane market. Through this strategic approach, Elite Property Law positioned themselves for success in property consulting, striving to attract more customers and improve their visibility on platforms like Google.

Task Flow

Plan & Pricing

Upon landing on Elite Property Law's homepage, users can easily navigate to the "Plan & Pricing" section to explore available services and pricing options. They can access this section directly from the main navigation menu or through a prominent call-to-action button strategically placed on the homepage. Within the "Plan & Pricing" section, users can browse through the various service offerings and their corresponding pricing details. Once they have reviewed the options, they can proceed to schedule a consultation or contact Elite Property Law for further assistance.

Consultation & Contact

Users have two convenient options for reaching out to Elite Property Law: leaving a message and personal details under the "Contact Us" section directly on the website, or scheduling a consultation via email using an external source. In the "Contact Us" section, users can fill out a form with their inquiry and contact information, allowing Elite Property Law to follow up with them promptly. Alternatively, users can opt to schedule a consultation by clicking on an email link provided on the website, which directs them to their preferred email client to initiate the appointment booking process. Both options offer users a seamless experience to engage with Elite Property Law and seek personalised assistance for their property-related needs.

Architecture

Elite Property Law's website architecture will consist of several key sections aimed at providing comprehensive information and seamless navigation for users. These sections will include:

Homepage: The central landing page introducing users to Elite Property Law, its core services, and client testimonials.

About: A section detailing the background, values, and expertise of Elite Property Law's team.

Services and Pricing: An overview of the various property law services offered by Elite Property Law, including detailed descriptions and transparent pricing information for Elite Property Law's services.

Contact: A section offering multiple contact options, including a contact form and scheduling a consultation via email.

User Testing & Feedback

Services & Pricing

Users found the task flow intuitive and user-friendly, particularly the easy access to the "Services & Pricing" section from the homepage.

The clear and concise presentation of information in the "Services & Pricing" section was appreciated, helping users understand the available services and associated costs.

Users who opted to schedule a consultation via email found the process seamless and appreciated the external link provided for easy access to their preferred email client.

Overall, the user testing feedback highlighted the effectiveness of the task flow in guiding users through the website, with minor enhancements suggested to further enhance the user experience.

UI Style Guide

Colour Palette

The primary colour palette for Elite Property Law consists of blue (#0C3C60) as the primary colour, complemented by grey (#F7F7F7) as the secondary colour. White (#FFFFFF) serves as the accent colour. These colours are chosen not only for their visual appeal but also for their high contrast and readability, enhancing usability.

Images

High-quality and professional images related to Elite Property Law are incorporated, complementing the deep blue and grey colour palette. Images are optimised for fast loading times without compromising quality, ensuring a smooth user experience.

Layout

A clean and minimalist layout is maintained, with ample whitespace to improve readability and visual appeal. Consistent spacing and alignment of elements are ensured for a cohesive look. The layout is designed to be responsive, adapting seamlessly to different screen sizes and orientations for optimal usability across devices.

Accessibility

Sufficient colour contrast for text and background elements is ensured to meet accessibility standards, along with the use of accessible fonts and font sizes for improved readability for all users. Additionally, all interactive elements are designed to be accessible via keyboard navigation and screen readers, ensuring inclusivity and usability for users with disabilities.

Branding

Elite Property Law's logo is prominently incorporated throughout the website, maintaining consistency with brand colours and typography across all pages. Branding elements are strategically placed to enhance brand recognition and trust while ensuring a cohesive and visually appealing user experience.

Typography & Buttons

Typography

Headings are presented in Brandon Grotesque, Arial, or sans-serif fonts for a clean and professional appearance, while body text is displayed in Avenir, Roboto, or sans-serif fonts for optimal readability. Font sizes and line spacing are carefully chosen to ensure ease of reading across different devices and screen sizes.

Buttons

Primary buttons feature a deep blue background with white text, while secondary buttons have a light grey background with black text, both during hover and click states. Both button types have a border of 1px solid matching their respective background colours. Buttons are designed with suitable padding and size to facilitate easy tapping on touch devices, enhancing usability.

Hover Effects

Subtle hover effects are utilised to indicate interactive elements, such as buttons and links, with slight colour changes or shadows to signify interactivity. These effects provide visual feedback to users, enhancing usability and improving the overall user experience.

Forms

Input fields utilise a deep blue background with white text. Submit buttons feature a white background with blue text. Input fields have clear focus states as the line will change from 1px to 2px to indicate user interaction, and form labels are placed above the fields for improved usability, especially on smaller screens.

Cards

Elite Property Law's website incorporates a cards section to spotlight essential information and services. These cards come in two sizes: small and medium. Small cards, set against a light grey background with black font, include a Heading 4, Heading 1, and Paragraph to clearly express important information. Medium cards follow the same colour scheme and contain a centrally aligned Heading 1, Paragraph, and button. This design strategy ensures uniformity and readability across all card sizes, facilitating user comprehension and interaction with the content provided.
