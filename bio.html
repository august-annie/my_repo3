Biography

As a UX/UI freelancer, my passion lies in problem-solving and creating user-centric solutions. Through my projects, I am committed to addressing real-world challenges by deeply understanding user needs and preferences. Leveraging my expertise in typography, persona creation, and human-computer interaction, I craft innovative designs that not only solve problems but also enhance the overall user experience.

In addition to my freelance work, I am the founder of LE_MUNCO Candles & Decor, where I apply the same problem-solving approach to ensure a seamless online shopping experience for customers. By integrating web design, photography, and video content creation, I strive to create engaging platforms that meet user expectations and preferences.

Outside of work, I am an avid badminton player and continuously seek to expand my knowledge in user psychology and human-computer interaction. My goal is to create products and experiences that resonate with users on a deep level, ultimately enriching their lives and addressing their needs.

Please feel free to explore my projects to see examples of how I've applied problem-solving and user-centric design principles to various projects. Thank you for your interest!
