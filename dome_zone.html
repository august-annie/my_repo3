Dome Zone

The Dome Zone Mental Health App is designed to simplify access to mental health support in Australia. Our user-centric approach prioritises simplicity and ease of use to break down barriers that often discourage people from seeking help. With features like detailed psychologist profiles and easy booking, our app empowers users to connect with the support they need effortlessly.

To measure success, we'll closely listen to user feedback, especially after therapy sessions. Positive responses, especially regarding improved well-being and satisfaction, will show how effectively the app enhances mental health support. With this innovative approach, we aim to make significant progress in addressing mental health challenges and building a happier, healthier society.

Challenges

A significant challenge for the Dome Zone Mental Health App is ensuring widespread acceptance and effective utilisation among Australians. Despite its user-friendly design, obstacles like stigma and limited awareness may persist. To address this, we must extensively promote the app and tailor it to meet the diverse needs of users. Collecting feedback post-therapy sessions will aid in comprehending its effectiveness and guide necessary enhancements. Through these endeavours, our goal is to enhance accessibility to mental health support for all Australians.

Solution

To make mental health support more accessible in Australia, the Dome Zone Mental Health App will focus on creating an easy-to-use platform. This means simple navigation and clear instructions for connecting users with suitable psychologists. We'll also promote the app widely to raise awareness. By listening to user feedback and making improvements, we aim to increase the use of mental health services and improve well-being for all users.

To establish Dome Zone as the leading mental health app in Australia by providing a seamless and user-friendly platform for busy individuals to find the right psychologist, ultimately increasing user engagement and retention.

Design Problem

After gathering user testing feedback from the low-fidelity prototype of the Dome Zone mental health app, we conducted a group discussion to prioritise the most critical and pressing issues for iterative design improvements.

Design Changes

In response to the feedback, we made several enhancements to improve the user experience. Firstly, we utilised the header space by introducing a circular progress bar icon, allowing users to track their current stage in the app's journey. Additionally, we implemented a 5-star rating system below each psychologist's name and education, ensuring that the star colours provide excellent accessibility contrast for all users. Furthermore, we adjusted the design's structure and layout to optimise screen space usage, enhancing overall usability and visual appeal.

Design Solution

In addition to addressing user feedback, we streamlined the user journey by consolidating unnecessary screens into a single, more efficient screen, eliminating redundancy and simplifying navigation. The calendar function was strategically relocated to the final booking stage, empowering users to seamlessly add appointment times to their local calendars. To enhance readability and visual coherence, we made adjustments to font size, text colour, and background colour throughout the app, ensuring a more cohesive and user-friendly UI design. Notably, we increased the font size on the home screen to prominently highlight that users can enjoy their first session with Dome Zone for free when booking their initial appointment, effectively communicating this valuable benefit to users.

Design Upgrade

We've enhanced the app's design by integrating appointment details with the Psychologist Bio screen, improving readability. Font sizes have been increased, and clearer colours have been used for better visibility. Users now have more information to review before booking appointments, including delivery methods and specialised fields, while the bio section remains uncluttered with only essential details. Furthermore, the selection of psychologists has been expanded, and swipe cards now offer additional information to assist users in making informed decisions. These updates are intended to create a more user-friendly and engaging experience for all Dome Zone app users.

Green Colour Palette

The chosen shade of green for the Dome Zone Mental Health app was purposefully selected due to its profound psychological benefits. Green is known to evoke feelings of tranquility, balance, and harmony, making it a calming hue that can alleviate stress and anxiety. Drawing from alternative healing practices such as chromotherapy, green light is believed to have a calming and balancing effect on the mind and body. Furthermore, the Biophilia Hypothesis suggests that humans have an inherent connection with nature, and exposure to natural elements like greenery can positively impact well-being, potentially leading to improved mental health and reduced anxiety.
