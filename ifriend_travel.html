iFriend

The iFriend travel app is meticulously crafted to address the challenges faced by individuals like William, who often find travel planning overwhelming and time-consuming. Through a user-centric approach, our goal is to create an intuitive and efficient application that streamlines the planning process, alleviating the stress associated with organizing trips.

In our user interviews, we discovered that users, including William, grapple with the plethora of travel information available, leading to decision fatigue and frustration. This underscores the importance of developing a solution that prioritizes user needs and presents relevant travel details in a clear and organized manner.

By focusing on enhancing the user experience, we aim to provide a seamless and stress-free planning experience for busy individuals like William, empowering them to efficiently plan and book their journeys with ease.
